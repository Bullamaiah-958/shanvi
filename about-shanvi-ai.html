About Shanvi AI Consulting

Shanvi AI Consulting helps businesses modernize operations, automate repetitive processes, and build intelligent systems that improve efficiency, customer engagement, and business growth.

Founded on a simple belief that modern businesses should not lose valuable time, leads, or opportunities because of outdated manual processes, we focus on delivering practical Artificial Intelligence solutions that create measurable business impact.

We work with organizations across Healthcare, Real Estate, Ecommerce, Coaching Institutes, Salons, and Small Businesses to streamline workflows, improve customer communication, automate routine tasks, and unlock operational excellence through AI-powered automation. Our expertise spans AI Strategy Consulting, WhatsApp Automation, Workflow Automation, AI Communication Systems, and Business Process Automation.

At Shanvi AI Consulting, we do not simply implement software. We design intelligent ecosystems that help businesses operate faster, communicate smarter, and scale efficiently. Whether you are looking to automate customer interactions, optimize internal operations, generate more qualified leads, or build AI-driven workflows, our solutions are designed to deliver real business outcomes.
